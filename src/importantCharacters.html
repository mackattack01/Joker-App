important characters

This Page will tell you about different characters which are important to the jokers story.

Name Summary Of Character
Batman The Joker's arch-nemesis and primary antagonist. Their relationship is one of mutual obsession, with the Joker seeing Batman as his perfect foil and often aiming to push him to his limits.
Harley Quinn (Dr. Harleen Quinzel) Initially introduced as the Joker's psychiatrist at Arkham Asylum, Harley Quinn becomes his loyal accomplice and lover. Their relationship is complex, often marked by manipulation, abuse, and codependency.
Commissioner James Gordon As Gotham City's police commissioner, Gordon frequently opposes the Joker's criminal activities. The Joker has targeted Gordon and his family multiple times to torment Batman and demonstrate his power over Gotham.
Barbara Gordon (Batgirl/Oracle) The Joker's attack on Barbara Gordon, where he paralyzes her in "The Killing Joke," is a pivotal moment that affects her character deeply and fuels Batman's resolve against the Joker.
Jason Todd (Robin/Red Hood) The Joker's brutal murder of Jason Todd, the second Robin, in "A Death in the Family," is one of Batman's greatest failures and profoundly impacts the Bat-family. Jason's later return as the anti-hero Red Hood adds another layer to their dynamic.
Alfred Pennyworth Batman's loyal butler and father figure often becomes a target in the Joker's schemes to hurt Batman emotionally.
