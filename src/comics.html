This Page will tell you about different versions of The Joker in DC comics.

Comic Year summary
The Killing Joke 1988 "The Killing Joke" follows the Joker's attempt to drive Commissioner Gordon insane by targeting his family. Batman races to stop him, leading to a confrontation that challenges the fine line between heroism and madness. The story also delves into the Joker's origins and his twisted philosophy on life.
Batman: The Man Who Laughs 2005 "Batman: The Man Who Laughs" depicts Batman's first encounter with the Joker in Gotham City. As the Joker spreads chaos with a deadly toxin, leaving victims with haunting smiles, Batman races to stop his reign of terror. The story explores the genesis of their iconic rivalry, highlighting the clash between Batman's vigilantism and the Joker's chaotic madness.
Arkham Asylum: A Serious House On Serious Earth 1989 "Arkham Asylum: A Serious House on Serious Earth" follows Batman's dangerous descent into the asylum, now under the control of its inmates led by the Joker. As Batman confronts the chaos within, the story delves into his inner struggles and the twisted psyches of Gotham's villains, exploring themes of madness and the thin line between hero and villain.
